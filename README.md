# FATHIMA-COLLECTIONS-Sales-Analysis-Report-2022-
🧾 Project Overview This project focuses on analyzing the annual sales data of FATHIMA-COLLECTIONS for the year 2022. The objective is to extract meaningful insights about customer behavior, sales performance, and business trends to help increase sales in 2023.
🎯 Business Objective
FATHIMA-COLLECTIONS store wants to:
•	Understand customer purchasing behavior 
•	Identify top-performing regions and categories 
•	Analyze sales trends 
•	Improve business decisions for 2023 growth 
________________________________________
❓ Key Business Questions
The analysis answers the following important questions:
•	📅 Which month got the highest sales and orders? 
•	👨‍👩‍👧 Who purchased more — Men or Women in 2022? 
•	📦 What are the different order statuses? 
•	📊 Compare sales and orders using a single chart 
•	🌍 Top 10 states contributing to sales 
•	👥 Relationship between age and gender 
•	🛒 Which sales channel contributes the most? 
•	🏆 What is the highest selling category? 
________________________________________
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
•	Power BI – Dashboard & visualization 
•	Excel – Data cleaning & preprocessing 
•	DAX – Calculations & measures 
________________________________________
📊 Dashboard Features
•	Monthly Sales & Orders Trend 
•	Gender-wise Sales Distribution 
•	Order Status Breakdown 
•	State-wise Sales Analysis 
•	Age vs Gender Analysis 
•	Channel-wise Contribution 
•	Category-wise Sales 
________________________________________
📈 Sample Insights
•	👩 Women contribute ~65% of total sales 
•	🌍 Top 3 states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh (~35%) 
•	👥 Adult age group (30–49 years) contributes ~50% 
•	🛒 Top channels: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra (~80%) 
________________________________________
🧠 Key Learnings
•	Customer segmentation is crucial for business growth 
•	Online platforms drive majority of sales 
•	Targeted marketing can significantly improve revenue 
________________________________________
🚀 Final Conclusion (Business Recommendation)
To improve sales in 2023, FATHIMA-COLLECTIONS should:
•	🎯 Focus on women customers, as they contribute the most 
•	📍 Target top-performing states with better marketing campaigns 
•	👥 Concentrate on adult age group (30–49 years) 
•	🛒 Strengthen partnerships with Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra 
•	📢 Use personalized marketing and offers
•	Conclusion
•	This project provides a clear understanding of sales patterns and customer behavior.
By leveraging these insights, FATHIMA-COLLECTIONS can make data-driven decisions to improve profitability and growth in 2023.
